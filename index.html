How to participate ?

Once you finish to play a two against two game, fill-up the form. The results will be recalculated and published whenever I fill like it, with no garantee of this being regular or reactive. Raw results (without ranking calculations) will be uploaded unregularly in Games. Only submit once per game ! Be careful to write your name correctly and consistently !

How does the individual ranking works ?

A homemade elo system that works for a 2vs2 game. Based on his post : https://math.stackexchange.com/questions/838809/rating-system-for-2-vs-2-2-vs-1-and-1-vs-1-game . If you are curious, go read the code on my github.

How does the team ranking works ?

Each team provide a partial ranking of the teams by looking at its average score against each team. A genetic algorithm is used to approximate a ranking that minimizes the collisions with all partial rankings. As I'm too lazy to give details, you can read my code. It's public after all. Two players are considered a team and ranked only after playing 4 games together. Each connected subset of teams will have its own ranking.

What if you did something wrong ?

The non-exaustive list being:

  • Submitting several times for the same game.
  • Submitting wrong informations.
  • Not writing your name correctly (shame on you).

If this happens, you can contact me on the whatsapp group of this tournament or my email. I might or might not help you depending on my mood.
